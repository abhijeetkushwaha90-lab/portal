SSB Preparation

Yeh general guidance hai jo SSB pattern samajhne ke liye hai. Exact format aapke recommending board/year ke notification ke hisaab se thoda different ho sakta hai.

WAT — Word Association Test

60 words ek-ek ke baad screen pe 15 second ke liye flash hote hain. Har word ke baad turant ek sentence likhna hota hai jo wahi word use kare. Yeh test spontaneous/natural reaction check karta hai — overthinking se Officer-Like-Qualities (OLQs) suppress ho jaati hain.

Tip: Positive, action-oriented aur simple sentences likhna best practice hai.

SRT — Situation Reaction Test

60 practical life situations diye jaate hain (30 min mein), jisme aapko ek realistic aur turant reaction likhna hota hai. Yeh decision-making, leadership aur practical intelligence judge karta hai.

Tip: Filmy/heroic reactions avoid karein — practical aur responsible jawab dein.

OIR — Officer Intelligence Rating

Two sets of verbal aur non-verbal reasoning questions, time-bound. Pattern recognition, series completion, coding-decoding, aur logical reasoning cover hota hai.

Tip: Speed + accuracy dono zaroori hai — daily practice rakhein.

GAT / GTO Tasks

Group Testing Officer tasks: GD (Group Discussion), GPE (Group Planning Exercise), PGT/HGT/CT (Group obstacle tasks), Lecturette, Command Task, aur Individual Obstacles. Yeh teamwork, initiative aur physical-mental coordination test karta hai.

Tip: Team mein contribute karna zaroori hai, sirf lead karna kaafi nahi.

Personal Interview

PIQ (Personal Information Questionnaire) form ke aadhar par 30-45 min ka one-on-one interview. Aapki background, hobbies, current affairs, aur self-awareness judge ki jaati hai. Honest aur consistent jawab dena sabse important hai — IO (Interviewing Officer) aapke PIQ se cross-question karte hain.

Tip: PIQ form bharne se pehle apne hi answers practice karein, har detail yaad rakhein.

Common Medical Rejection Reasons
  • Flat foot / Knock-knee / Bow legs (severe cases)
  • Untreated dental cavities, gum disease
  • Hernia, Varicocele, Hydrocele
  • Asthma, chronic bronchitis, TB history
  • Color blindness (especially Air Force ground/flying duty)
  • Overweight/Underweight (BMI outside acceptable range)
  • Deviated Nasal Septum (DNS) — usually correctable, can be temporary unfit
  • Visible/disallowed-area tattoos

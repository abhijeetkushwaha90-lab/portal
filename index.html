PPDT Winning Strategy
👤 Hero Rules
  • • Hero hamesha positive aur active hona chahiye
  • • Age 18-30 rakho
  • • Mood: determined, calm, confident
  • • Ek hi hero — group nahi
📝 Story Rules
  • • 4 parts: Hero → Problem → Action → Result
  • • Ending hamesha positive
  • • 80-120 words ideal
  • • Real life se inspired
🗣️ Discussion Rules
  • • Apni story clearly present karo
  • • Dusron ki baat suno
  • • Common story banana hai — fight nahi
  • • Leader ki tarah contribute karo
❌ Avoid Karo
  • • Death, murder, suicide mention mat karo
  • • Negative ending mat likho
  • • Bhoota ya supernatural mat likho
  • • Blank mat chhodo

TAT Story Writing Formula
✍️ Perfect Story Structure:
1 Past: Hero kaun hai, background kya tha
2 Present: Abhi kya situation hai
3 Action: Hero ne kya decided aur kiya
4 Future: Kya positive result aaya
✅ Example Story:
"Arjun (24) ek dedicated engineering student tha jo defence join karna chahta tha. Ek din usne dekha ki ek bachcha nadi mein dub raha hai. Bina der kiye woh paani mein kud gaya aur bachche ko bacha liya. Uski bravery ko dekh kar lokl log ne uski tarif ki aur woh apne sapne ki taraf aur motivated ho gaya."
